Activities in Bakuriani

Bakuriani offers a wide range of exciting activities for all seasons. Whether you're an adrenaline junkie or looking for family-friendly fun, there's something for everyone. This charming resort town, nestled in the mountains, is renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty and vibrant outdoor culture.

Winter Sports

Experience skiing, snowboarding, and ice skating on the picturesque slopes of Bakuriani. The area features well-groomed runs suitable for all skill levels, from beginners to advanced athletes. Take lessons from professional instructors, or explore the scenic trails on your own. In addition to skiing, enjoy cozy après-ski spots where you can relax and warm up with delicious Georgian cuisine.

Summer Hiking

Explore the beautiful hiking trails that wind through lush forests and scenic landscapes. With trails ranging from easy walks to challenging hikes, you can immerse yourself in nature while enjoying stunning views of the surrounding mountains. Guided tours are available for those looking to learn more about the local flora and fauna, and don’t forget to pack a picnic to enjoy at one of the many picturesque viewpoints.

Family Activities

From snow tubing to horse riding, enjoy various activities perfect for families and children. The family-friendly resorts in Bakuriani offer snow parks where kids can safely play and learn to ski in a fun environment. In the summer, take advantage of the mild weather to go horseback riding through the scenic countryside or enjoy a day at the local amusement park, which features rides and attractions for all ages.

Cultural Experiences

Engage with the rich cultural heritage of the region through various festivals and local events throughout the year. Experience traditional Georgian music and dance performances, visit local artisans, and sample authentic cuisine in nearby restaurants. Don’t miss the chance to explore the historic sites and museums that tell the story of Bakuriani's past and its significance as a mountain resort.

Wellness and Relaxation

Take a break from the excitement and indulge in some well-deserved relaxation. Bakuriani offers several wellness centers and spas where you can enjoy massages, saunas, and wellness treatments. Rejuvenate your body and mind in the serene mountain environment, making it the perfect destination for a peaceful getaway.
