Activities in Bakuriani

Bakuriani offers a wide range of exciting activities for all seasons. Whether you're an adrenaline junkie or looking for family-friendly fun, there's something for everyone.

Winter Sports

Experience skiing, snowboarding, and ice skating on the picturesque slopes of Bakuriani.

Summer Hiking

Explore the beautiful hiking trails that wind through lush forests and scenic landscapes.

Family Activities

From snow tubing to horse riding, enjoy various activities perfect for families and children.
