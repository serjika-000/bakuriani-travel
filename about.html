About Bakuriani

Bakuriani is a beautiful town nestled in the heart of Georgia, known for its breathtaking landscapes and diverse recreational activities. Whether you're visiting for winter sports or summer hiking, Bakuriani has something for everyone.

Historically, Bakuriani served as a winter training ground for Soviet Olympic athletes, and today it continues to attract visitors from around the world. From cozy cottages to luxury hotels, Bakuriani offers a range of accommodations to suit every traveler.

Discover the natural beauty of the region, explore the lush forests, or enjoy some family fun on the slopes. With year-round activities and a warm, welcoming community, Bakuriani is truly a destination for all seasons.
